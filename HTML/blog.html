Current Dream car

The 2021 Mercedes-AMG GT Black Series

This is my current dream car

An AMG Black Series is very rare. The 720-hp AMG GT is the sixth version of the AMG and Mercedes collaboration on AMGs own car. Some specs of the car include; • Engine Twin-Turbocharged 4.0-liter V8. • Output 720 Horsepower / 590 Pound-Feet. • Transmission Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch. • Drive Type Rear-Wheel Drive. • Speed 0-60 MPH 3.2 Seconds. • Maximum speed 202 MPH. • Efficiency 15 City / 20 Highway / 17 Combined. • Weight 3,500 Pounds This car is the automobile for someone who wants to be able to drive there car everyday to work and the racetrack on the weekend and be confident they will destroy most other cars there. Another great thing about the AMG Gt black Series is that Mercedes allows maximus customer customization with their bespoke package.
